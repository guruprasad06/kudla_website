About Namma Kudla

Namma Kudla is a celebration of Mangalore’s soul — its people, culture, cuisine, and coastal charm. Our platform brings together the stories and sights that make Mangalore one of Karnataka’s most captivating destinations.

Whether you’re here to explore its serene beaches, ancient temples, or bustling food streets, Namma Kudla is your guide to experiencing the city like a local — with warmth, authenticity, and pride.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote sustainable tourism in Mangalore while preserving its rich heritage. We aim to inspire travelers to discover the beauty of Kudla responsibly — supporting local businesses, respecting traditions, and protecting the natural environment that makes our region special.

What We Stand For

Authenticity

We share genuine experiences that reflect the heart of Mangalore, from local traditions to hidden destinations.

Community

We believe tourism should uplift local communities and celebrate their stories and livelihoods.

Sustainability

We promote mindful travel practices to preserve the natural beauty and cultural legacy of Kudla.

Discovery

Our goal is to inspire curiosity and exploration — beyond the beaches, into the heart of Mangalore’s identity.
